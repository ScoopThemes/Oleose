Meet Flightbites

Order food while traveling and pick it up at the airport. No lines, no hassel.

Easy

Just scan your boarding pass and Flightbites will show you restaurants at your destination or departure airport.

Fast

Restaurants receive your order on real time, making your food available as quickly as possible.

Convenient

Completely cashless so you only have to worry about your flight, not keeping track of your wallet.
